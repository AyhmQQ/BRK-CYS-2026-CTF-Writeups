Welcome, contestant.

In this challenge, you will dive into the depths of an unforgettable tragedy, where the threads of fate intertwine with the complexities of modern cryptography.

"The Gwen Stacy Protocol" is not just a challenge — it is a journey through three stages of loss and despair.

Each time you connect to this protocol, all parameters will change. Every attempt is unique. Any mistake will disconnect you instantly, and upon reconnecting, you will face a completely new instance.

Are you ready to face the loss?

---

Structure of the challenge:

Stage 1 — Memory of Threads
- Chaotic Elliptic Curve Cryptography
- Dynamic parameters (p, a, b)
- Time-based seeds

Goal:
Decrypt a message to obtain the key for the next stage.

---

Stage 2 — Gwen’s Fall
- Learning With Errors (LWE)
- Random matrix A
- Secret vector s
- Noise e

Equation:
b = A·s + e

Goal:
Recover the secret vector s.

---

Stage 3 — Peter’s Solitude
- Dual chaotic systems (Logistic + Tent maps)
- State poisoning
- Temporal interleaving

Goal:
Use the recovered data to decrypt the final flag.

---

One-shot rule:
- Any wrong answer → immediate disconnect
- Reconnect → completely new challenge

Good luck.
